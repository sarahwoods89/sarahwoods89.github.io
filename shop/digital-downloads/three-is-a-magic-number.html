Three is a Magic Number

How do you eat an elephant? One bite at a time.

A 12 week planner for anyone who has written a to do list as long as their arm, then got so overwhelmed they never started. Hand written in red Sharpie on dotted paper, in the same Sarah Words style as the prints.

Each week opens with a hope to hold on to. Monday to Thursday you choose three things to prioritize, Friday makes room for the three things you didn't get to (and that's ok), and the weekend is left for play, family, friends, space and rest. Every week closes with a watch and a read recommendation, a little company along the way.

No mood trackers, no water intake box. Just three things, every day, for twelve weeks.
