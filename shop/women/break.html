Break

Some viewers will see a pair of knickers and immediately attach a sexual connotation. In fact, this painting could never be promoted on socials and was often reported and flagged for violating guidelines? Eh, it's an image of a woman sitting on a toilet with only one shoe on.

Originally painted with memories of the women's bathrooms on nights out in the early 2000s, it reflects a space of messy craic, a safe space for bitchin' and compliments in equal measure, and a brief and welcomed break from the outside bar.

This painting is a limited edition raising funds for the Dublin Rape Crisis Centre. 50% of the profits will be donated.
