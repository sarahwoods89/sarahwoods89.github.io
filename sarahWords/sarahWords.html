The Sarah Words collection lives primarily on chain. Starting out as a web3 collection, sold on Coinbase's layer 2, BASE through HUG, it has evolved to real life pieces as well. Each dollop of paint connects with a word, allowing the viewer to read a little bit slower than normal. Something I feel is important to really hear the words being read. In a world full of opinions and noise through so many mediums, I am trying to say something in as little words as possible in an aesthetically pleasing way.
