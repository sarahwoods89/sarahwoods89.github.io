Terms & Conditions

Last updated: April 2026

1. Prints & Production

All prints are produced on Hahnemühle fine art papers by The Copperhouse Gallery in Dublin. Each print is made to order and produced to the highest archival standard.

Limited edition prints are individually numbered and hand signed by the artist. Once an edition sells out, no further prints of that size will be made.

Open edition prints are hand signed by the artist.

2. Packaging & Shipping

All prints are carefully rolled and posted in a sturdy protective tube to ensure they arrive in perfect condition. We take great care in packaging every order before it leaves us.

Shipping rates and estimated delivery times are displayed at checkout. We ship from Ireland.

3. Damage in Transit

While every care is taken in packaging your print, once it has been collected by the courier we are unable to accept responsibility for any damage that occurs during transit.

In the unlikely event that your print arrives damaged, please photograph both the outer packaging and the print immediately upon opening and contact us at sarahwoodsartist@gmail.com. We will do our best to assist you and will work with the courier on your behalf where possible.

4. Returns & Refunds

As all prints are made to order, we are unable to accept returns or offer refunds unless the print arrives damaged or there has been an error on our part. Please contact us within 7 days of receipt if there is an issue with your order.
